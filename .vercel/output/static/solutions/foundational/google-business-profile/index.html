Common Questions About Google Business Profile

Get answers to the most common questions about our Google Business Profile services.

By claiming your profile, you gain control over the information displayed by Google. Without claiming your listing, Google may inaccurately present details about your business, potentially leading to misinformation and missed sales opportunities.
Regaining access can take some extra steps such as providing additional documentation to prove your ownership. We have experience working with Google to get new owners properly verified, ensuring you have full control of your digital presence.
