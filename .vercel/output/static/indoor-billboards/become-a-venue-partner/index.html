Become A Venue Partner

Our network of indoor digital billboards is located within retail and restaurant venues across your local community. Venue partners enjoy the benefits of discounted screen advertising, cross-network promoting and much more! Check out our current network locations to see where your venue could be featured.

Why Do Venues Want to Join Our Network

01

Network Advertising

One of the most enticing advantages of the network is that your venue's motion ads will be showcased in nearby non-competitive locations, enabling you to reach out to customers of other venues. Some of these customers might also be yours, thus enhancing your top-of-mind brand awareness, increasing frequency, and sparking word-of-mouth referrals through our screen advertising network.

02

Internal Advertising

Participating in the network also provides you with the opportunity to engage with your customers while they are inside your establishment. Many venues utilize screen time to inform, promote, or educate their customers about ongoing specials, promotions, or helpful tips.

03

Content Creation

We handle all graphic content creation for our Venue Network Partners. Just provide us with a few design elements, perhaps some pictures and messages, and we'll take it from there.

04

Access to Marketing Services

Being part of our network also provides you with access to a variety of different marketing services and our highly talented, trained marketing staff who manage them. We cover everything from websites and social media to pay-per-click advertising, geofencing, and everything in between.

05

Local Support

Whether it's a simple technical or maintenance question, a content ad change, or an inquiry about different marketing campaign options, we are here to meet with you and service your needs.

06

More Than Ads

We go beyond continuous promotion of your business within the screen network, offering exceptional brand reach. We also provide engaging content for viewers, including weather updates, trivia, and fun facts, creating a more enjoyable experience for customers.

How do I Become a Venue Partner?

02

03
